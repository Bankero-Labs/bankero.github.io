Banking made simple
Modern banking, powered by stablecoins and designed for global access.

Open a Free Account Instantly: Get started with just a smartphone, leveraging stablecoin technology for secure, fast account setup.
Multi-Currency Wallets: Seamlessly manage fiat, crypto, and stablecoins for flexibility in global transactions.
Connect and Manage All Your Bank Accounts: Use Open Banking to integrate your existing accounts into Bankero and view your entire financial landscape in one app.

Spend Smarter
Make every purchase count with powerful cashback rewards and spending tools.

Cashback Rewards: Earn instant cashback on every purchase, directly to your Bankero wallet. Use it to save, reinvest, or spend again—your rewards, your way.
Exclusive Merchant Discounts: Unlock special offers and savings when you shop with Bankero's partner brands across categories like travel, dining, and retail.
Real-Time Expense Tracking: Know where your money goes with detailed spending analytics categorized across all your accounts.

Send money Anywhere, Anytime
Fast, transparent, and hassle-free global payments.

No Fees, No Stress: Send money to family, friends, or anyone across the globe without worrying about hidden charges. What you see is what you send.
Instant Transfers That Actually Feel Instant: Whether it’s across the table or across the world, your payments move as fast as your plans.
Send in Any Currency, Without the Guesswork: Pay or transfer in over 30 currencies, with real-time exchange rates so you always know exactly what you’re paying.

Save and Grow Your Money
Making saving easy, rewarding, and just part of life.

Earn While You Save: Get daily interest on your savings—no minimums, no lock-ins, just steady growth.
Save Effortlessly: Every time you spend, we round up your purchases to the nearest dollar and automatically save the difference.
Reach Your Goals: Set a goal—like a vacation, a rainy day fund, or something special—and watch your savings grow toward it.

Invest with Confidence
Simple, accessible investment options for everyone—no experience needed.

Start Small, Grow Big: Invest as little as $10 in curated portfolios that match your goals, whether you’re saving for the future or looking for steady returns.
Tokenized Real-World Assets: Own a fraction of real estate, businesses, or other tangible assets, all securely powered by blockchain technology.
Monitor and Learn as You Go: Track your investments in real time with easy-to-understand insights, and get educational tools to help you grow as an investor.

Protection and Peace of Mind
Your payments, your data, and your finances—protected by blockchain and built for modern convenience.

Secure Payments with Virtual Cards: Use single-use virtual debit and credit cards for secure online and in-store purchases. Eliminate risks of fraud with cards that are tailored for every transaction and expire after use.
Effortless Contactless Payments: Pay anywhere with virtual cards or tap-and-go NFC technology, ensuring every transaction is fast, seamless, and secure.
Your Assets, Always Protected: With end-to-end encryption and blockchain validation, your money, personal data, and tokenized assets are safeguarded from cyber threats.
Emergency Credit, When You Need It: Instantly access credit lines secured by your assets, without lengthy processes or middlemen.

Effortless Payments and Global Lifestyle
Making your money work for you—anywhere in the world.

Pay Like a Local, Anywhere: Use real-time exchange rates to spend in over 30 currencies, whether you’re at home or abroad. No hidden fees, just fair and transparent transactions.
Tap-and-Go Convenience: Pay with your phone or virtual card for secure, contactless transactions—no need for outdated POS systems.
